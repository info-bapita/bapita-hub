### 4. Non-Payment, Cancellation, and Data

**Non-payment:**  
A payment reminder will be sent. After 7 business days without payment, the page will be suspended. Client data will be retained for 30 additional days, then permanently deleted.

**Cancellation by Client:**  
Written notice required (WhatsApp message counts) with 30 days advance notice. The last paid monthly period will remain active until it expires. No refund on the setup fee.

**Cancellation by Service Provider:**  
30 days written notice. No further monthly fees will be charged beyond the active period.
